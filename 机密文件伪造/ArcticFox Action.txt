主要内容

通过北极进入西伯利亚，在荒原中找到能量阀，并向基地传回坐标。尽力不要造成伤亡。如果可能，请渗透到敌方深处，获取实验数据，设计图等机密内容并带回基地。
如果暴露，无路可逃就自杀。不能让敌方掌握情报。如果你发现队友的叛变，请毫不犹豫将他杀死。

任务开始你们将成为幽灵，你们的档案将会注明死于二战，只有你们的直属上司知道你们的身份。

Enter Siberia through the North Pole, find the energy valve in the wasteland and send coordinates back to base. Do your best not to cause casualties. If possible, infiltrate the enemy's depths, get the experimental data, design drawings and other confidential contents and bring them back to the base.
If exposed, kill yourself if there is no way to escape. Do not let the enemy have the information. If you find your teammate's mutiny, please do not hesitate to kill him.

At the beginning of the mission you will become ghosts, your files will state that you died in World War II, and only your immediate superiors will know your identity.
